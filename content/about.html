Who I Am

I’m a full stack trained developer with a passion for the front end and UI/UX. I’ve developed and designed games, mashups, weather apps, as well as mockup work for a non-profit organization. Along with my work experience, I tutor web development students in the MERN stack with Trilogy Education Services.

Before getting into the world of developing, I was in the media field, having worked as both a journalist and a broadcast services specialist. After realizing my interest in web development, I decided to pursue a path that would allow me to expand upon my passion for user interface and experience.
